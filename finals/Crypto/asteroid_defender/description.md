Challenge Title: `Asteroid Defender`<br>
Author: `Ivann`<br>
Description:<br>
Our asteroid early-warning AI has malfunctioned.The auto-shield system is offline.

The radar CAN still detect incoming asteroids and report their unique "Trajectory Signature", a raw value output from the station's
onboard prediction engine.

Each asteroid strikes one of 8 sectors: N, NE, E, SE, S, SW, W, NW.

If someone could predict the next 100 sectors in a row, we can manually re-calibrate the shields and save the station.

Are you good enough to read the future?
