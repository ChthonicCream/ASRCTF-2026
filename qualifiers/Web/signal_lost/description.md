Challenge Title: `SignalLost`<br>
Author: `hzy`<br>
Description:<br>
The year is 2147. Earth's deep-space relay network spans fourteen star systems, but communication with Waystation Orrery — a crewed research outpost orbiting a rogue planet beyond the heliopause — has been unstable for weeks. Solar interference, they said. Routine degradation, they said.
You're not so sure.<br>

A civilian uplink portal has been left open for family members and contractors to transmit short text messages to the crew aboard Orrery. The station's automated message handler receives each transmission, formats it into the station's internal log format, and echoes a delivery confirmation back to the sender.
The crew hasn't responded in eleven days. The last outbound signal from Orrery was fragmented — just noise, and a single word: help.
You have access to the uplink portal. The system is old. It was built fast, by a contractor who has since moved on. Nobody has audited it.<br>

Maybe the silence isn't interference. Maybe something on the station — or someone — is watching the messages come in.
Send a transmission. See what comes back.<br>
