Challenge Title: `Cosmic Leak`<br>
Author: `Ivann`<br>
Description:<br>
Ground Control has noticed suspicious behavior from one of our orbit navigation engineers, suspected of working with a competitor.
Security logs indicate that a large transmission was sent to an external server right before they went offline. 

We tapped the network interface and intercepted a packet capture of the transmission (`space_launch_leak.pcap`).
The transmission seems to include standard rocket telemetry and a launch cover photo.
Rumor has it the engineer hid classified rocket launch payloads inside the photo itself.
