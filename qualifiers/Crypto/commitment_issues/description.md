Description:<br>
CryptoVault Inc. has deployed a new commitment scheme for their secure
lottery protocol. Before the draw, each participant commits to their chosen
number. After the draw, they open the commitment to prove they picked
correctly all along.<br>

The scheme is built on "industry-standard discrete-log hardness" over a
carefully chosen modulus. The developers were proud enough to publish the
parameters.<br>

Something about this modulus doesn't sit right.<br>
